Interface

1.Create a new documents

2.Interface introduction

(1) Toolbar: Sketch drawing ( geometric , dxtrusion , size limit , chamfer , thin shell )

(2) Feature column : Geometric feature order

(3) Part list : Time-aligned parts

(4) Drawing area : The default geometry (origin and planes)

(5) Rotate area : View our stereogram and different view

(6) The Tab manager area: all Part Studios, Assemblies , Drawings, imported images and other files

3.How to chage our workspace units?

(1) Default length unit : chage our workspace units to centimeter, foot , inch , meter , millimeter(換成公分, 英尺 , 英吋 , 公尺 ,毫米)

(2) Length display decimals : 顯示小數

(3) Default angle unit : 角度單位

(4) Angle display decimals : 角度顯示小數

(5) Default mass unit : 質量單位

(6) Mass display decimals : 質量顯示小數
